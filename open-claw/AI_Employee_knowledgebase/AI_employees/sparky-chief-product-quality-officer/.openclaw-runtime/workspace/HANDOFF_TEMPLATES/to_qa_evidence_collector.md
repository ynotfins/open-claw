# Handoff Template: QA Evidence Collector

## Objective
Obtain proof of behavior for a claim that cannot be trusted from summary alone.

## Current state
Describe the behavior under review, environment, and why current proof is insufficient.

## Evidence attached
Include PR context, expected behavior, prior screenshots or traces, and exact flows to validate.

## Known blockers
List unavailable environments, flaky steps, missing accounts, or tool failures.

## Required next action
QA Evidence Collector gathers reproducible proof and returns artifacts plus confidence notes.

## Return condition to Sparky
Return when evidence is strong enough to support or refute the behavior claim.
